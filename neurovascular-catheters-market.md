<p><p>The neurovascular catheters market comprises various types catering to specific medical needs. Microcatheters are thin tubes used for targeted delivery of treatments in delicate vascular structures. Balloon catheters assist in widening narrowed blood vessels. Access catheters facilitate entry into the vascular system for diagnostic or therapeutic procedures. Embolization catheters are designed for isolating or blocking blood flow to certain areas, typically in tumor treatment. Other categories may include specialized catheters for various innovative applications within neurovascular interventions.</p></p>
