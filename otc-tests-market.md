<p><p>The OTC (over-the-counter) tests market encompasses various types of diagnostic tools, including immunoassays and lateral flow assays. Lateral flow assays, commonly seen in rapid tests, involve a simple sample application that produces visual results, such as lines on a strip. Both types offer convenient, user-friendly options for at-home testing, promoting self-diagnosis and timely health decisions.</p></p>
